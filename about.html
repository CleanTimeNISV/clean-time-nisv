About Us

Jinay Shah - CEO and Founder: Jinay has been passionate about watch care from a young age. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, Jinay established Clean Time to provide top-notch watch cleaning services.

Ehimaya Oza - Co-Founder: Ehimaya combines expertise in technology with a love for horology. His innovative approaches to watch maintenance and service have greatly contributed to Clean Time’s success.

Aahan Choudhary - Co-Founder: Aahan brings a wealth of experience in customer service and operations. His dedication to ensuring every customer’s satisfaction has been instrumental in the growth of Clean Time.

How It All Started

Jinay Shah, always fascinated by timepieces and their care, came up with the idea to help others keep their watches in perfect condition. Recognizing that this service could be both valuable and affordable, he reached out to his close friends Ehimaya Oza and Aahan Choudhary. Together, they founded Clean Time, driven by a shared passion and a clear goal: to offer premium watch cleaning services at a price that’s easy on the wallet.

Our mission is simple – we believe that everyone should be able to afford high-quality care for their watches. With our combined expertise, dedication, and customer-first approach, we ensure that every watch gets the attention it deserves. At Clean Time, affordability doesn't mean compromising on service; it means delivering excellent results at a price everyone can appreciate. We hope you give us the chance to keep your treasured watches in their best condition!
